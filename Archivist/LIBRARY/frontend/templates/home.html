Library Rules

General guidelines for maintaining decorum and proper usage of library facilities.

Silence & Decorum

Strict silence must be maintained in the reading rooms. Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.

Bags and personal books should be kept at the counter. The library is not responsible for lost items.
